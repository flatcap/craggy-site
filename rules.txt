Craggy Ten Commandments

1.	Stay outside the red lines unless climbing or belaying
2.	Don.t walk through the arches
3.	Never pull, stand or put fingers in the bolts, chains or quickdraws
4.	Stay below the max feet line unless you are tied in and belayed
5.	Always remove your harness before bouldering
6.	NEVER walk under a climber
7.	Always climb down on the bouldering wall, NO jumping off
8.	Remember to check your partner before you climb
9.	Always listen to your instructors directions, they are there to help you
10.	A high standard of behaviour is expected at all times
