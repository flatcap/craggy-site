Rules

Craggy Ten Commandments

Centre
	* Walk around the red lines, belayers and ropes
	* Don't walk under the arches
	* Keep below the max feet line unless belayed
	* Don't hold or stand on metal rings, chains or quickdraws

Safety/Behaviour
	* Check your partner before you climb
	* Try to belay within the red lines
	* Don't swing on the ropes

Bouldering
	* Remove your harness before bouldering
	* Don't climb near, or underneath, other boulderers
	* Don't jump down, try to climb down safely

Smallprint
	A high standard of behaviour is expected at all times
	Always listen to your instructors directions, they are there to help you
